# Ntsiniz — Store Listing Copy (Draft)

## Short description

Audio-first singing coach. Train drills, track progress, keep your best takes.

## Full description

Ntsiniz helps you sing sharper with fast, focused drills.

- **Audio-first coaching**: tune accuracy, stability, intervals, slides, and echo drills.
- **Proof over vibes**: every attempt gets a score and a progress trail.
- **Best take saved**: Ntsiniz marks your best attempt per drill so you can track real improvement.
- **Playback with waveform**: replay takes and tap-to-seek through your performance.
- **Offline by default**: drills and training history live on your device.

### Privacy note (store-safe)

Your microphone is used only to run drills and record your takes. Training data is stored locally on your device. You can choose to share images/exports using your phone’s share sheet.
