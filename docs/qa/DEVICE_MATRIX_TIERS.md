# Device Matrix (Tiered)

We validate the app on **representative devices** per tier.

Target market mix:

* **High-end (40%)**
* **Mid-range (50%)**
* **Low-end (10%)**

> Pick exact models based on your market. Below are examples.

## High-end (HIGH)

| Platform | Example models | OS |
|---|---|---|
| iOS | iPhone 15 / 15 Pro | iOS 17+ |
| Android | Galaxy S23/S24, Pixel 8 | Android 14+ |

## Mid-range (BALANCED)

| Platform | Example models | OS |
|---|---|---|
| iOS | iPhone 12 / 13 | iOS 16+ |
| Android | Galaxy A54/A55, Pixel 7a | Android 13+ |

## Low-end (LITE)

| Platform | Example models | OS |
|---|---|---|
| Android | Galaxy A13/A23, Moto G series | Android 12+ |

## Required coverage

* At least **1 device per tier** per platform where available.
* At least **2 Android mid-range** devices (vendor variance).
* At least **1 noisy-room test** and **1 quiet-room test** per tier.
