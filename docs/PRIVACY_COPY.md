# Ntsiniz — Privacy Copy (Draft)

## What we access

- **Microphone**: used to capture audio while you practice drills.
- **Local storage**: used to store your sessions, attempts, and best-take markers on your device.

## What we store

- **On-device training history**: scores, drill metrics, and session summaries.
- **On-device audio takes**: recorded takes are saved as WAV files in the app cache. Your device/OS may clear cache files.

## Sharing

If you choose to share a progress card or drill result, Ntsiniz uses your device’s **system share sheet**. The app does not automatically post anything.

## Network / accounts

- No account is required.
- By default, your training data remains on your device.

## Data deletion

- Uninstalling the app removes the local database.
- Cache audio files may be removed automatically by the OS.
