# Ntsiniz Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-02-26  
**Last updated:** 2026-02-26  

This Privacy Policy explains how **Ntsiniz** ("Ntsiniz", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our mobile application, websites, and related services (collectively, the **"Service"**).

> **Plain-English promise:** your recordings are created on your device. You control what you share. We aim to collect the minimum needed to run the Service.

---

## 1) Who we are & how to contact us

**Operator:** Ntsiniz  

For privacy questions or requests, use the in-app Support option (Settings → Support) or the contact method listed on the store page for your build.

---

## 2) What we collect

We collect information in three main ways: (A) information you provide, (B) information created through your use of the Service, and (C) information from third parties.

### A) Information you provide

- **Account information** (if you create an account): display name, username/handle, email/phone (if used), profile photo, and optional profile details.
- **User content**: posts, comments, competition submissions, marketplace feedback requests/replies, and messages you choose to include.
- **Support communications**: what you send us (e.g., bug reports, emails).

### B) Information created through your use

- **Practice & performance metrics**: drill results, scores, pitch/stability/timing metrics, streaks, mission progress, and similar training analytics.
- **Recordings & media**:
  - **On-device recordings**: audio/video clips you record are stored locally on your device by default.
  - **Shared media**: if you publish a clip/post/competition submission or share a link, the related media becomes accessible to others according to the sharing context.
- **Device and app data**: device model, OS version, app version, language/locale, and basic diagnostics.
- **Usage events**: feature interactions (e.g., started drill, completed drill, tapped share) to improve reliability and experience.

### C) Information from third parties

- **Subscriptions / purchases**: if you purchase Pro, we receive entitlement status and purchase metadata from our payment partners.
  - Current provider: **RevenueCat** (which connects to Apple App Store / Google Play). We do not receive your full payment card details.
- **App store platforms**: Apple/Google may provide refund status, subscription status, and region.
- **Crash reporting**: if enabled, crash reports may come from a provider like **Sentry**.

### Analytics & advertising

Ntsiniz is **not an ads business**.

- We do **not** sell your personal information.
- We do **not** use third‑party advertising SDKs.
- We do **not** collect advertising identifiers for targeting.
- We may use **crash reporting** (Sentry) and basic diagnostics **only if you enable it** in Settings.

For the definitive list of third-party services enabled in production builds and what they receive, see `docs/privacy/DATA_MAP.md`.

---

## 3) Microphone, camera, and files permissions

Ntsiniz requests access to:

- **Microphone**: to record practice and performance audio.
- **Camera** (optional): to record performance video.
- **Media/files** (optional): to let you import/export clips and share packs (e.g., duet packs, coach feedback packs).

We do **not** continuously record in the background. Recording happens when you explicitly start a recording session.

---

## 4) How we use information

We use information to:

1. **Provide the Service**
   - run drills, calculate scores/grades, show your progress
   - enable posts, comments, competitions, duets, and marketplace features
2. **Personalize & improve**
   - recommend missions, next drills, and discovery content
   - debug and improve app performance
3. **Safety & trust**
   - respond to reports, prevent abuse, enforce community standards
   - maintain audit logs for moderation actions
4. **Payments & entitlements**
   - confirm Pro access and restore purchases
5. **Communications**
   - send service messages (e.g., account, security, important updates)
   - marketing emails/notifications (only if you opt in)

## 5) Legal bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

If you are in the EEA/UK, we process personal data under these legal bases:

- **Contract**: to provide the Service you request (e.g., run drills, show progress, deliver Pro features).
- **Legitimate interests**: to improve reliability, prevent fraud/abuse, and secure the Service.
- **Consent**: where required for optional features (e.g., marketing, certain analytics).
- **Legal obligation**: to comply with applicable laws.

Note: jurisdiction-specific requirements depend on your launch regions. If you expand beyond your initial launch regions, review this section with counsel.

## 6) How we share information

We share information only as needed:

### A) With other users

- If you post content publicly, other users can see it.
- If you participate in competitions/leaderboards, your display name and submission may be visible.
- If you use offline sharing packs (duet/coach feedback), the recipient will receive whatever you included in the pack.

### B) With service providers

We may share data with vendors that help run the Service, such as:

- **Payment/entitlements**: RevenueCat + App Store / Play Store
- **Crash reporting**: Sentry (if enabled)
- **Hosting / storage (optional)**: Supabase (only if cloud sync is enabled)

Providers are required to protect information and process it only for our instructions.

### C) For legal reasons

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

### D) Business transfers

If we’re involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred as part of that deal.

---

## 7) Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary:

- **On-device recordings**: remain on your device unless you delete them or share them.
- **Shared content**: remains available until you delete it (or we remove it for policy reasons).
- **Moderation records** (reports/audit logs): retained for safety and compliance.
- **Purchase/entitlement records**: retained for accounting and fraud prevention.

Retention note: on-device recordings remain on your device unless you delete/share them. Cloud-synced data (if enabled) follows the retention policy in your backend configuration.
