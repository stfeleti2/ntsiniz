# Ntsiniz Community Guidelines

**Effective date:** 2026-02-26  

Ntsiniz is a place to practice, share progress, and enjoy singing together. These guidelines explain what’s allowed and what isn’t.

If content violates these guidelines, we may remove it and take action (warnings, suspensions, bans).

---

## 1) Be respectful

- No harassment, bullying, threats, stalking, doxxing, or hate speech.
- No sexual harassment or unwanted sexual content.
- No impersonation.

---

## 2) Keep it safe

We do not allow:

- content that encourages self-harm or violence
- extremist propaganda
- instructions for wrongdoing
- sexual exploitation of minors

If you see a safety issue, use **Report**.

---

## 3) Content rights (copyright)

Only upload content you have the right to share.

Examples:
- ✅ your own singing performance
- ✅ public domain works
- ✅ content you have permission to use
- ❌ copyrighted music recordings you do not own the rights to distribute

Backing tracks/covers: comply with applicable copyright rules in your region. If you add a covers feature, present any additional rules in-app.

---

## 4) Privacy

Do not share:

- someone else’s private information (phone numbers, addresses, IDs)
- private recordings of others without permission

---

## 5) Spam & manipulation

Not allowed:

- spam comments, repetitive posts, or misleading links
- manipulation of likes/leaderboards with bots or coordinated spam

---

## 6) Competitions and fairness

- No cheating or exploiting scoring.
- No submitting content that isn’t yours.
- We may disqualify entries that violate rules.

---

## 7) Reporting and moderation

Use **Report** to flag:

- abusive posts/comments
- harassment
- explicit or illegal content
- impersonation
- cheating

We review reports and may take action. We may also keep audit logs of moderation actions for safety.

---

## 8) Enforcement

Actions may include:

- content removal
- warning
- temporary suspension
- permanent ban

Appeals: if your build includes moderation actions, an appeal process may be provided in-app or via Support.
